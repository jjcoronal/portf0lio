## About me

> _I like to think about the broad systems in the world and understand how they impact us as different scales. Through visual frameworks, participatory methods, and facilitation I’m looking to help organizations turn scattered information into narratives that can enact new world dynamics._

> _I work across three scales, from the conceptual and theoretical, to participatory methods, to the implementation of real, data-driven design. I bring all three together, alongside stakeholders, to push innovation past the pilot stage into lasting change. Throughout, systems thinking, sustainability, and human-centered design are a constant lens._
